V0.0.0
Changelog Summary
Fixed tutorial navigation and page progression issues.
Improved grammar, labels, headers, and instructional text across TAM, SAM, SOM, CSP, OPEX, and Funding pages.
Standardized terminology, including renaming CSP to “Intended Pricing”.
Enhanced dashboard calculation interdependency and real-time data reflection.
Improved Funding page UI with combined repayment fields, reduced cell widths, dynamic funding balance, and number-to-words support.
Improved responsiveness and usability on smaller screens.
Removed textbox spinner arrows and disabled unnecessary autofill behavior.
Added persistent navigation controls, glowing completed-step indicators.
Improved visual consistency with spacing fixes, continuous arrow animation, and non-empty placeholder states.
